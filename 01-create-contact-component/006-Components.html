Component

We have created all the code in the App component. That does not make it reusable or maintainable, so it is good practice to divide the logic into smaller and reusable components. For this, there are two conceptual types of components:

  • Containers: contain logic and state related to the app, and handles other components. They are also known as Pages
  • Components (presentational): they only have logic related to themselves, and their data and state are inserted only by properties. They are totally reusable. They are also known as Dummy components

Our AppComponent is a container. Now we'll create a presentational component (ContactComponent).
