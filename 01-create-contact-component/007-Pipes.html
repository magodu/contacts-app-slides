Advanced: Pipes

Pipes are helpful for formatting output in our templates.

Many of the pipes provided by Angular 2 will be familiar if you've worked with filters in Angular 1.x.

Pipes are accessed in our templates in the same way that filters were: with the "pipe" character |

You can see the pipes provided by Angular 2 in the Angular 2 website

We can build our own custom Pipes. We'll see how to build a custom pipe to format an US phone number.
